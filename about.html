About Career Builder College

Founded: 2006

Address: 3, Alabaja Street Iyedi-Titun Ota Ogun State.

Brief History

Career Builder College, Ota, was established with a passion to provide quality, affordable, and value-driven education. Since September 2006, the college has remained committed to academic excellence, discipline, innovation, and moral uprightness.

Vision

To be a leading institution committed to nurturing disciplined, innovative, and socially responsible learners equipped for excellence.

Mission

  • Provide high-quality, value-driven education.
  • Develop intellectual and leadership capacity.
  • Promote excellence and continuous improvement.
  • Foster critical thinking and creativity.
  • Build responsible citizens grounded in integrity.

Core Values

  • Excellence
  • Integrity
  • Innovation
  • Character Development
  • Student-Centeredness
  • Leadership
  • Community Engagement
